Full Stack Developer

Grow into a full stack developer role with deeper expertise across both backend and frontend layers, contributing meaningfully to system architecture and design decisions.

Action Plan

01 Learn a modern JavaScript framework such as Vue.js or React

Progress to a senior developer or technical lead position where I can mentor junior developers, contribute to system design decisions, and lead development teams effectively.

Action Plan

01 Deepen expertise in software architecture, design patterns, and system scalability
02 Develop leadership and project management skills through formal training

04 Contribute to open-source projects and build public visibility as a developer
